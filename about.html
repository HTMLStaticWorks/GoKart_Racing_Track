OUR MOTORSPORT HISTORY

What started as a single outdoor racing track has developed into a premium multi-facility motorsport hub. We were among the first to commit fully to high-performance electric powertrains, eliminating emissions while tripling initial torque outputs.

Our facility is designed by professional racing architects. It merges safety run-off loops with technical chicanes and elevation switches, offering professional karting to families and competitive leagues alike.

THE RACING FLEET

Our fleet features state-of-the-art electric powertrains providing instant acceleration.

Powertrain

Electric Drive

400cc equivalent brushless motors with remote speed overrides for total safety controls.

Chassis

Chromoly Steel

Low center of gravity race frames with impact-absorbing bumpers for G-force traction.

Telemetry

Live transponders

Transponders capture split-seconds at every sector, displaying timings to the lounge board.

High-density fast charge batteries offering 45 minutes of continuous full power runs.
