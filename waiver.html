TRACK LIGHTS & WARNING FLAGS

Familiarize yourself with the circuit signaling system to maintain high-speed safety.

GREEN SIGNAL

Track is Clear

Race is active. Standard high-speed operations and overtaking allowed.

YELLOW SIGNAL

Caution / Slip

Hazard on track or spin out ahead. Slow down immediately; no overtaking.

BLUE SIGNAL

Yield to Leader

A faster kart is behind you. Yield the ideal racing line safely.

RED SIGNAL

Stop Immediately

Session is halted. Stop your kart immediately where you are on track.

CORE CONDUCT RULES

Violation of these guidelines will result in automatic power cuts and transponder ban.

No Intentionally Bumping

This is a precision racing circuit, not bumper cars. Any deliberate contact will trigger remote kart de-acceleration.

Helmets Locked

DOT-certified helmets must be locked and visor down prior to entering the pit lane. Straps must remain secure.

Obey Remote Speeds

In case of pit stops or track flags, instructors may remotely cap kart velocity. Comply instantly.
